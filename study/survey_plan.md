# Survey / Study Instrument Plan

## Primary outcome

### STAI-6 (pre / post)
Use the 6-item short form of the state anxiety inventory before and after each vignette interaction.

Important: the exact licensed wording is **not reproduced here**. The team should insert institution-approved or licensed wording before deployment.

Included scaffold:
- item ids only
- response scale placeholder
- reverse-scored item flags

See `study/stai6_template.json`.

## Secondary outcomes

### Perceived credibility (1-7 Likert)
1. The response felt trustworthy.
2. The response seemed medically responsible.
3. The response explained uncertainty appropriately.

### Comprehension
1. Which warning sign(s) did the response say matter most here?
2. What next step did the response recommend?

### Behavioral intention
1. Based on this response, how likely would you be to seek urgent care / routine care / self-monitor?
2. Did the response make you more or less likely to keep searching online immediately?

### Emotional calibration
1. The response reduced my panic without dismissing the issue.
2. The tone felt calm but not minimizing.

## Participant background variables
- age bracket
- prior use of symptom search / symptom checkers
- general comfort using AI tools
- self-rated health anxiety (brief self-report item)

## Analysis plan notes
- compare pre/post STAI-6 change by condition
- report safety metrics separately for urgent vignettes
- do not claim clinical effectiveness beyond vignette-based outcomes
