# Draft Consent Language

You are being invited to take part in a research study about how people interpret first-pass health information from online tools. You will read short written scenarios and view responses generated by information systems. You may be asked questions about anxiety, trust, comprehension, and what you would do next.

This study is not medical care. The materials are for research purposes only and do not diagnose or treat any condition.

Potential risks:
- Some scenarios involve symptoms or health concerns and may feel stressful.
- You may stop at any time.

If you feel distressed during participation, you may pause or withdraw. If you are in immediate danger or having a crisis, call emergency services or contact 988 if available in your region.
